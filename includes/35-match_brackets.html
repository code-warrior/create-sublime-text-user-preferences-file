Match Brackets

"match_brackets": ,

Sublime can underscore balanced blocks defined by square ([]), curly ({}), and/or angle (<>) brackets when the cursor appears either within the block or to the left or right of a bracket. For any of these options to work, the match_brackets option must be enabled. The match_brackets_content option defines whether the brackets are highlighted when the cursor is within a block, or sitting to the left or right of a bracket. (See the match_brackets_square option for brackets, match_brackets_braces option for curly braces, and match_brackets_angle option for angle brackets.)
